# PAF 4 — Vicensae System & Protocol

> **"We do not store files; we encapsulate truth."**

## Vision
PAF 4 is a **Fiduciary Custody Regime**. In this ecosystem, work is not a fragmented asset in a third-party cloud; it is a **Vector Cinema** scene preserved within a **High-Stability Judicial Container (HSJC)**. 

Under the **VICENSÆ** protocol, we ensure that the author’s declared intent remains immutable, legible, and sovereign across time and platforms.

---

## System Architecture

The architecture is built on the strict separation between **Access Governance** and **Semantic Custody**:

* **/hsjc**: **The Canonical Container.** Manages manifest sealing, lineage, and rendering conditions. It is the atom of the system.
* **/ingest**: **The Foundry.** Extracts intent, validates assets, and produces the HSJC artifact.
* **/vault**: **The Sanctuary.** Provides armored custody and integrity sealing specifically for HSJC containers.
* **/catalog**: **The Ledger.** Manages relationships, indexing, and the global state of the collection.
* **/viewer**: **The Projector.** A high-fidelity engine that renders directly from HSJC specifications.
* **/auth**: **The NotTR Regime.** Governance of identity and admission (RAGO / Poimandres).
* **/api**: **The Neural Link.** Secure communication layer between modules.

---

## Axial Principles: Regime vs. Container

* **Access is governed by the NotTR regime**: Identity and admission are validated through a non-arbitrary governance layer.
* **Truth is preserved by HSJC**: The work is encapsulated in a self-describing, verifiable container that ensures rendering fidelity.
* **Fiduciary Sovereignty**: Control belongs to the author; the infrastructure is merely the custodian.
* **Tending the Garden**: *"Flowers do not ask for permission to bloom, but someone must tend the garden for it to endure."*
